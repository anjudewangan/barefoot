Your Path to Mastery

Program Structure

Course
Week 1

28th - 29th Sep 2024

Awareness & Prevention

  • Introduction to the course
  • Identifying potential dangers and threats in different environments
  • Situational awareness techniques
  • Body posture and confidence projection
  • Basic blocking and defense techniques for common threats (e.g., wrist grabs, choke holds)
  • Mental conditioning and response
Week 2

5th - 6th Oct 2024

Escapes & Counterattacks

  • Techniques for breaking free from grabs and holds (wrist grabs, hair pulls, bear hugs, etc.)
  • Learning to identify weak points of an attacker
  • How to strike effectively: palms, elbows, knees
  • Stances and how to maintain balance while defending
  • Scenariobased practice with partners
Week 3

12th - 13th Oct 2024

Advanced Defense Techniques

  • Dealing with attacks from the front, side, and behind
  • Defense against armed attackers (knives, blunt objects)
  • Ground defense and escapes
  • Learning how to fight back and disengage
  • Protecting yourself in enclosed spaces (car, small rooms)
  • Group practice : Simulating reallife scenarios
Week 4

19th - 20th Oct 2024

Real World Applications & Situational Defense

  • Defense against multiple attackers
  • Defending yourself in a confined space
  • Protecting someone else (children, friends)
  • Practice sparring with controlled intensity
  • Psychological aspects of selfdefense
  • Confidencebuilding exercises and discussion
Week 5

Extended Course (Weeks 5 - 8)

Extended Course

  • Deeper focus on advanced techniques
  • 26th 27th Oct 2024 : Specialized drills for highstress environments
  • 2nd 3rd Nov 2024 : Fighting with improvised weapons (e.g., keys, purse, pens)
  • 9th 10th Nov 2024 : Repeated situational sparring with live simulations
  • 16th 17th Nov 2024 : Fitness drills to increase agility, strength, and endurance for selfdefense
Week 6

Program Extras

  • Customized Self Defense Plan : After the course, participants will receive personalized feedback on areas they need to work on and strategies for selfdefense.
  • Self Defense Kit : A free personal alarm or pepper spray as part of the extended course.
  • Certification of Completion : Upon successful completion, participants will receive a certificate from Barefoot Metabolics.
